Circa (from Latin, meaning 'around, about'), usually abbreviated c., ca or ca. (also circ. or cca.), means "approximately" in several European languages including English, usually in reference to a date.[1] Circa is widely used in genealogy and historical writing when the dates of events are not accurately known.
